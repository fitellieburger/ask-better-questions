What is Ask Better Questions?

Ask Better Questions helps you slow down and see how a piece of writing works — not whether it’s right or wrong.

  • Words — how labels and phrasing shape interpretation
  • Proof — what the text shows, checks, or asks you to accept
  • Missing — what standards, limits, or context are left unstated

What the meter means

The meter shows grounding, not truth. Fill reflects support in this text. Glow reflects heat — how strongly the author commits to a take.

Heat ≠ support.

How to use it

Read the questions or cues, then go back to the article. If you can answer them easily, the piece is doing its job.
